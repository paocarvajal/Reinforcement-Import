MTC Quantity & Identification Coverage:

An MTC for X tons of a specific thickness can cover multiple shipments, as long as the total imported quantity does not exceed X tons. For shipments containing different reinforcement types or from multiple original steel mills/fabricators, each type/source must be clearly identifiable within the shipment, and its corresponding MTCs provided.

Mandatory Mill Test Certificate (MTC) Contents

To ensure compliance with Mexican import regulations, each Mill Test Certificate (MTC) for the raw steel material *per thickness* must contain the following details:

1. Chemical Composition

  • Carbon (C) %
  • Silicon (Si) %
  • Manganese (Mn) %
  • Phosphorus (P) %
  • Sulfur (S) %
  • Other specified elements %

2. Mechanical Properties

  • Tensile Strength (Tension)
  • Yield Strength
  • Elongation (%)
  • Hardness (if applicable)

3. Traceability Information

  • Unique Coil Number
  • Unique Heat Number
  • Batch/Lot Number
  • Date of Production

4. Physical Dimensions & Coverage

  • Nominal Thickness
  • Actual Thickness
  • Width
  • Weight (per coil/batch)
  • Total Tonnage/Weight Covered by this Certificate

5. Standard Adherence

  • Declaration of compliance with international standards (e.g., ASTM A653/A653M or EN 10346:2015 for DX51D +Z100).

6. Origin & Manufacturer

  • Name, Address, and VAT number of the original steel mill.
  • Confirmation that no Russian raw materials were used in the steel production.

By providing these comprehensive Mill Test Certificates, Veratec enables NeoProfile to comply fully with Mexican import regulations, ensuring efficiency and avoiding customs delays. This meticulous documentation strengthens our shared commitment to quality and a seamless supply chain.
